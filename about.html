Brief History & Meet our Staff

Supreme Fitness GYM (SFG) was established in June of 2015 for the sole purpose of helping to keep people healthy through exercises.

Three very important persons played major parts in the establishment of SFG in so far as providing up to US$200,000.00 as initial amount. This fund was used to rent our original office and gym, which were situated in Bargain Avenue, New Orlean, purchase equipment, pay electricity and other bills, etc.

During our first year of operation, we had a little financial problem, especially dealing with salaries of our employees. Now, we are operating with ease. We owe all of these to the Lord Almighty who has given us the ability and wisdom to thrive.

At SFG, we have only one thing on our mind: To help you our customers become healthy by keeping you fit. Thanks.

It is good to know the people you are going to be dealing with when you come to Supreme Fitness GYM. These are our dynamic staff.
